WHAT WE OFFER

Our Goal: Promote digital inclusivity among seniors by providing targeted education and training on computer, smartphone, and tablet usage, helping them to overcome technological barriers and reduce social isolation.

Mobile Phones

Learn and recieve support on how to better use your smartphone. Includes: home screen customization, using Google Translate, customizing Widgets, using Siri/Google Assistant, etc. Much more awaits!

Tablets

Learn and recieve support on how to better use your tablet. Includes: home screen customization, using Siri/Google Assistant, using writing styli, etc. Much more awaits!

Laptops

Learn and recieve support on how to better use your laptop. Includes: app customization, Zoom/Google Meet communication, etc. Much more awaits!
